Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to make our services work correctly and to improve your experience over time.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality; cannot be disabled without affecting service quality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site in aggregate, allowing us to improve content and performance.

You can manage, limit, or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling certain cookies may affect availability or functionality of parts of our services. We do not use cookies for cross-site advertising or third-party behavioural tracking.
